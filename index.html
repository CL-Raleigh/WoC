Way of the Cross

In Downtown Raleigh.

The idea of a Way of the Cross around the streets of Downtown Raleigh was born to help ourselves to be immersed in the Passion of Christ, that the Church invites us to meditate on Good Friday, and second as a missionary gesture for the city we live in. The procession will make stops throughout downtown Raleigh. At each station, we will follow the traditional readings from the Gospel and supplement them with choral songs and short passages from modern Catholic writers like Paul Claudel and Charles Peguy.

The Way of the Cross will start and finish at Sacred Heart Church, 200 Hillsborough St Raleigh, NC 27603. The procession will make three stops throughout downtown Raleigh.
On Good Friday, April 7th. We will start at 10.30 am and conclude by 1.00 pm.
